About Manani Inc.

My clothing brand is about making absolutely fabulous clothing while staying true to our original goal: keeping the environment clean, healthy, and making clothes sustainably. The style of the clothing that we create is modern, minimalistic, and branded clothes that have a meaning. We achieve this by using sustainable materials like cotton and avoiding unsustainable materials and overproduction. We also advocate for workers' rights in less economically developed countries.

Brand Name and Its Meaning

The reason for my brand name is because my nickname is Manani, which is what my mother calls me occasionally. “Manani” means blessing, representing how nature is a blessing and should be cared for. This name has personal significance as it is used across my online presence.

Target Audience

Our target audience is people who care about the environment. The age range is roughly 15–30 years old. Customers are expected to be at least middle class, as sustainable clothing is more expensive than fast fashion.

Products

We sell sustainable and ethical clothing for environmentally conscious people. Our range includes jackets, t-shirts, shirts, jumpers, and branded pants. The style is minimalistic rather than trendy. Accessories are limited; for example, beanies may be included, but we avoid tote bags or hats.

Affordability

Our products are not as affordable as fast fashion brands due to the cost of sustainable materials and ethical manufacturing practices. Sustainable fabrics like cotton are more expensive than fossil-fuel-derived materials like polyester, and wages in ethically managed factories are higher.

Thought Behind the Clothing

All clothing is designed with environmental awareness, sustainability, and ethical production in mind. Messages include caring for the environment, promoting equal rights, and avoiding war. Minimalism is chosen for personal preference and inspiration from family members who wear minimalist styles.
