The Design Process of Your Label

Firstly, I looked for inspiration centered around the environment, since the main focus of my company is sustainability and environmental friendliness. I thought of a peace sign incorporating trees, the sun, and oceans to reflect this theme. Secondly, I chose a cursive handwriting font that feels organic and natural to me. Lastly, I added wavy lines for style and included details like size and material on the neckline tag.

Why Did You Design It This Way?

The peace sign represents peace, respect for the environment, and sustainability. Trees symbolize deforestation issues, oceans highlight increasing pollution, and the sun represents global warming caused by trapped heat from greenhouse gases like CO₂. Combining these elements within the peace symbol shows that protecting our planet is directly connected to creating a peaceful, balanced world for future generations.

Platform Used for Design

I used Canva for my logo and neckline tag design. Canva offers a wide array of elements that streamline the design process, including automatic snapping, which makes logo creation quick and easy. A minor downside is that some features require a paid subscription.

How Does Your Label Represent You?

My label represents me because I deeply care about protecting the environment from destruction. Sustaining the environment is vital to prevent issues such as global warming, rising sea levels, and polluted air causing health problems. The peace symbol not only reflects environmental sustainability but also my anti-war beliefs, as I oppose violence caused by political greed.
