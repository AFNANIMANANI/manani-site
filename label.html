The Design Process of Your Label

I drew inspiration from the environment, focusing on sustainability. I created a peace sign using trees, the sun, and oceans, chose a natural cursive font, and added simple details like size and material to the tag.

Why Did You Design It This Way?

The peace sign represents sustainability and balance. Trees, oceans, and the sun highlight issues like deforestation, pollution, and global warming, showing the importance of protecting the planet.

Platform Used for Design

I used Canva to design the logo and tag. It’s easy to use and offers many elements, though some features require payment.

How Does Your Label Represent You?

My label reflects my commitment to protecting the environment and reducing issues like pollution and climate change. It also represents my belief in peace and a better future.
