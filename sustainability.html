What is Sustainability?

Sustainability means looking after the environment so it is protected for the future. This includes animals, plants, the atmosphere, and more. We do not use unsustainable materials like nylon, polyester, or acrylic. Instead, we use sustainable materials such as organic cotton, linen, and hemp, which are plant-based and renewable. We encourage avoiding fast fashion companies, as their practices worsen pollution and waste.

What is Fast Fashion?

Fast fashion is a strategy used by companies like Temu or Shein to mass-produce clothing cheaply. They often exploit cheap labor and use unsustainable materials. Unsold items frequently end up in landfills or are burned, releasing harmful chemicals into the environment.

How is Manani Inc Sustainable?

We use materials such as cotton, linen, hemp, and mushroom leather, which are plant-based and renewable. We manufacture in China, using advanced technology and ensuring fair wages. We only produce limited quantities to reduce waste, and leftover clothing is upcycled into new products.
