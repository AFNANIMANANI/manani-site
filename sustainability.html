What is Sustainability?

Sustainability means looking after the environment so it is protected for the future. This includes animals, plants, the atmosphere, and more. We do not use unsustainable materials like nylon, polyester, or acrylic. We avoid fossil fuel-derived materials and instead use sustainable materials like organic cotton, linen, and hemp. These materials come from plants and can grow again, making them sustainable. We encourage avoiding clothing from unsustainable or fast fashion companies, as purchasing from them worsens pollution.

What is Fast Fashion?

Fast fashion is a monetization strategy that companies like Temu or Shein use to produce large amounts of clothing in bulk. They often use cheap, unsustainable materials and exploit cheap labor in countries like Bangladesh or India. These practices are inhumane and unsustainable, creating mass amounts of clothing, not all of which is purchased. Unsold items often go to landfills or are burned, releasing toxic chemicals into the environment.

How is Manani Inc Sustainable?

We use materials such as cotton, linen, hemp, and mushroom leather. These materials come from plants and are able to grow again, making them sustainable.

We manufacture our clothing in China because of advanced technology and fair wages for workers. We do not overproduce; only a limited number of pieces are sold at a time (e.g., 30-50 pieces). Leftover clothing is upcycled into new pieces, reducing waste and supporting sustainability.
